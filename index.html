About Me


My name is Christine Dang. I am a 24 year old from Tampa, Florida. After graduating from the University of Florida in 2017 with a degree in Business Innovation and Sociology, I took 2 years off and managed 2 bakery locations - one in Gainesville and the other in Tampa - all while managing their social media and their sister restaurant's 7 chain online customer care service. During this time, I also volunteered at UF's Neurosurgery lab and Pediatric Physical Therapy clinic, took graduation photos for students, and bartended wedding events on the side.


My hobbies include: Playing piano, guitar, viola, cello, ukelele, and clarinet, playing Video Games, creating digital cartoon art drawings, portrait and landscape drawings, painting, spray painting, etc. via traditional art mediums,photography & videography, testing new recipes in the kitchen, & cake sculpting (Like Ace of Cakes and Cake Boss)
