Mission Statement:

About Me


My name is Christine Dang and I am from Tampa, Florida. I am a full stack web developer, UX/UI designer, and graphic illustrator with a business acumen.

After graduating from the University of Florida with a degree in Sociology and Business Innovation, I took 2 years off and worked as the regional manager for Sweet Buns Bakery, LLC (Gainesville and Tampa) all while managing their social media and their sister restaurant's 7 chain customer care service. During this time, I also volunteered at UF's Neurosurgery lab and Shands Hospital Pediatric Physical Therapy clinic, took graduation photos for students, and bartended wedding events.

I am now working as a UX/UI researcher & designer and web developer, all while continuing my education in UX/UI design to bring clients the best product possible.
