About Me


My name is Christine Dang and I am from Tampa, Florida. I am a full stack web developer and a graphic illustrator with a business acumen.

After graduating from the University of Florida with a degree in Business Innovation and Sociology, I took 2 years off and worked as the regional manager for Sweet Buns Bakery, LLC (Gainesville and Tampa) all while managing their social media and their sister restaurant's 7 chain customer care service as a board member.

I also volunteered at UF's Neurosurgery lab and Pediatric Physical Therapy clinic, took graduation photos for students, and bartended wedding events during this time.
